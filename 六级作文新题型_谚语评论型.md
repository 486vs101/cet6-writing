# 六级作文新题型：谚语评论型（Proverb Commentary Type）

---

## 一、写作模板框架

### 题型特征

题目给出一句谚语/名言（如 "Rome was not built in a day."），要求考生对该观点表明立场并展开论述。核心逻辑是：
- 首段：引出话题 + 亮明态度 + 嵌入谚语
- 中间段：论证谚语蕴含的道理
- 尾段：总结升华

### 模板框架

#### 首段（P1）— 唯一需要按题改编的段落

> Currently, the topic — **[主题概括]** — has triggered widespread concern and heated discussions among the public. Personally, I fully agree with this view, as saying goes "**[谚语原文]**."

**模板结构拆解：**

| 成分 | 固定句式 | 可变占位符 |
|---|---|---|
| 背景引入 | `Currently, the topic —` | `[主题概括]` |
| 社会关注 | `has triggered widespread concern and heated discussions among the public.` | 固定 |
| 态度表态 | `Personally, I fully agree with this view,` | 固定（亦可根据立场改为 `disagree`） |
| 嵌入谚语 | `as saying goes "` | `[谚语原文]` |

#### 中间段（P2）— 论证段（使用已有模板）

首句以 `I` 开头，展开论证谚语道理的正确性。通常包含：
- 论点句：为什么这个道理成立
- 例证：名人/历史/生活例子支撑（如 Da Vinci 画鸡蛋 → Mona Lisa）
- 对比：东方价值观（敬业精神、普通人也很重要）vs 西方个人英雄主义

可用的论证角度（来自笔记提示）：
- **基础技能的重要性**：万丈高楼平地起，基础不牢则大厦将倾
- **积累的力量**：量变引起质变，参考 Da Vinci 画鸡蛋的故事
- **敬业精神（professional dedication）**：做好分内小事
- **东方集体价值观**："普通人也很重要，一个也不能少" vs 西方"屌丝一定要逆袭"

#### 尾段（P3）— 总结升华（使用已有模板）

重申谚语的指导意义，发出倡议或展望。

## 二、改编套用方法

### 核心原则：**谚语题只改首段**

P2 和 P3 使用已有成熟模板，无需大幅改动。唯一需要根据题目定制的是 P1 的**两个占位符**。

### 改编步骤

**Step 1：确定主题概括词**

将谚语的核心内涵抽象为一个名词短语，填入 `[主题概括]`。

| 谚语 | 主题概括 |
|---|---|
| Rome was not built in a day. | the accumulation / down-to-earth attitude |
| Practice makes perfect. | the importance of repeated practice |
| No pains, no gains. | the relationship between effort and reward |
| Actions speak louder than words. | the value of taking real action |
| Honesty is the best policy. | the significance of integrity |
| Knowledge is power. | the transformative role of education |
| A friend in need is a friend indeed. | true friendship in times of adversity |
| Where there is a will, there is a way. | the power of determination and perseverance |

**Step 2：填入谚语原文**

将题目给出的谚语直接填入 `"[谚语原文]"` 位置，注意保留引号内的原句。

**Step 3：微调态度词（可选）**

绝大多数题目要求同意该谚语，用 `fully agree`。极少数反问式题目可能需要 `disagree` 或 `partially agree`。

### 套用示例

#### 示例 1：Practice makes perfect.

> Currently, the topic — **the importance of repeated practice** — has triggered widespread concern and heated discussions among the public. Personally, I fully agree with this view, as saying goes **"Practice makes perfect."**

#### 示例 2：Actions speak louder than words.

> Currently, the topic — **the value of taking real action** — has triggered widespread concern and heated discussions among the public. Personally, I fully agree with this view, as saying goes **"Actions speak louder than words."**

#### 示例 3：Honesty is the best policy.

> Currently, the topic — **the significance of integrity** — has triggered widespread concern and heated discussions among the public. Personally, I fully agree with this view, as saying goes **"Honesty is the best policy."**

#### 核心论证思路（中文）

1. **基础技能是根本**：任何伟大成就都建立在扎实基本功之上。达·芬奇从画鸡蛋开始，最终创作出《蒙娜丽莎》。
