## 一、原文范文（主题：终身学习）

### P1 — 引言段

> In the digital age, it is vitally important to develop lifelong learning. Personally, I fully agree with this view.

### P2 — 论证段一（举例论证）

> Undoubtedly, lifelong learning serves as a powerful weapon in the ever-changing society. For example, plenty of middle-aged workers keep learning digital devices and skills after work to avoid being eliminated by updated technology. Therefore, lifelong learning can help them keep competitiveness amid fierce social competition.

### P3 — 论证段二（双维度分析）

> The significance of lifelong learning manifests in two key dimensions. Firstly, regarding personal growth, it enables middle-aged and elderly people to enhance self-awareness, and clarify personal positioning and goals. Secondly, in the context of career development, it facilitates gaining a competitive edge, and accumulating resources for future development, thereby demonstrating its multifaceted value.

### P4 — 结论段

> In conclusion, we must realize the significance of lifelong learning. Therefore, individuals should develop relevant awareness and start from small things; besides, governments are advised to introduce more courses and activities to promote national lifelong learning. Were we to make concerted efforts, everyone could achieve further progress.

## 二、模板框架完整拆解（四段式）

### 模板一：引言段（Introduction）

**功能**：背景引入 + 亮明观点

| 步骤 | 功能 | 模板语言 |
|------|------|----------|
| Step 1 | 时代背景铺垫 | In the **[时代/背景]** , |
| Step 2 | 引出议题 | it is vitally important to **[议题/行为]** . |
| Step 3 | 亮明个人立场 | Personally, I fully agree with this view. |

**模板骨架**：

> In the [时代/背景], it is vitally important to [议题/行为]. Personally, I fully agree with this view.

---

### 模板二：论证段一（Body Paragraph 1）— 举例论证

**功能**：定性断言 → 举例说明 → 因果推导

| 步骤 | 功能 | 模板语言 |
|------|------|----------|
| Step 1 | 断言定性 | Undoubtedly, **[议题]** serves as a powerful weapon in **[领域/环境]** . |
| Step 2 | 举例说明 | For example, **[具体群体]** keep **[行为表现]** to **[目的]** . |
| Step 3 | 因果推论 | Therefore, **[议题]** can help them **[达成效果]** amid **[环境]** . |

**模板骨架**：

> Undoubtedly, [议题] serves as a powerful weapon in [领域/环境]. For example, [具体群体] keep [行为表现] to [目的]. Therefore, [议题] can help them [达成效果] amid [环境].

---

### 模板三：论证段二（Body Paragraph 2）— 双维度价值分析

**功能**：总述意义 → 维度一展开 → 维度二展开 → 收束总结

| 步骤 | 功能 | 模板语言 |
|------|------|----------|
| Step 1 | 总述意义（双维度预告） | The significance of **[议题]** manifests in two key dimensions. |
| Step 2 | 维度一展开 | Firstly, regarding **[维度一]** , it enables **[群体]** to **[行为A]** , and **[行为B]** . |
| Step 3 | 维度二展开 | Secondly, in the context of **[维度二]** , it facilitates **[行为C]** , and **[行为D]** , |
| Step 4 | 收束总结 | thereby demonstrating its multifaceted value. |

**模板骨架**：

> The significance of [议题] manifests in two key dimensions. Firstly, regarding [维度一], it enables [群体] to [行为A], and [行为B]. Secondly, in the context of [维度二], it facilitates [行为C], and [行为D], thereby demonstrating its multifaceted value.

---

### 模板四：结论段（Conclusion）

**功能**：重申观点 → 分主体建议 → 虚拟语气展望

| 步骤 | 功能 | 模板语言 |
|------|------|----------|
| Step 1 | 重申重要性 | In conclusion, we must realize the significance of **[议题]** . |
| Step 2 | 主体一建议 | Therefore, **[主体一]** should **[建议一]** ; |
| Step 3 | 主体二建议 | besides, **[主体二]** are advised to **[建议二]** . |
| Step 4 | 虚拟语气展望 | Were we to make concerted efforts, **[受益群体]** could achieve further progress. |

**模板骨架**：

> In conclusion, we must realize the significance of [议题]. Therefore, [主体一] should [建议一]; besides, [主体二] are advised to [建议二]. Were we to make concerted efforts, [受益群体] could achieve further progress.

## 三、模板改编套用方法总结

### 3.1 通用改编五步法

| 步骤 | 操作 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 1 | **确定主题词** | 全文统一替换 `lifelong learning` 为你的核心论点关键词 |
| 2 | **替换时代背景** | `digital age` → 选择与主题匹配的时代特征词 |
| 3 | **替换举例群体与场景** | `middle-aged workers` → 受该议题影响最直接的群体 |
| 4 | **替换双维度标签** | `personal growth` + `career development` → 贴合主题的二元维度 |
| 5 | **替换结论建议主体** | `individuals` + `governments` → 主题相关的两方责任主体 |

### 3.2 双维度灵活切换表

模板三的"维度一 + 维度二"可根据主题灵活选择以下任意二元对立组合：

| 使用场景 | 维度一（微观/个人） | 维度二（宏观/社会） |
|----------|---------------------|---------------------|
| 通用 | personal growth | career development |
| 教育类 | academic performance | character development |
| 科技类 | personal convenience | social efficiency |
| 环保类 | individual health | social sustainability |
| 文化类 | personal cultivation | cultural exchange |
| 经济类 | personal finance | economic growth |
| 理论实践 | theoretical significance | practical application |
| 短期长期 | short-term benefits | long-term influence |

### 3.3 结论段分主体建议替换表

| 主题类型 | 主体一（微观） | 主体二（宏观） |
|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 学习/教育 | students / individuals | schools / educational institutions |
| 环保 | citizens | enterprises / governments |
| 科技 | users | tech companies |
| 健康 | patients / residents | hospitals / health authorities |
| 就业 | job seekers | employers / companies |
| 社会问题 | individuals / families | governments / communities |

### 3.4 进阶改编技巧

| 改编维度 | 操作方法 | 示例 |
|----------|----------|------|
| **维度数量扩展** | 从 two → three dimensions，增加 `Thirdly, in terms of [维度三], it contributes to …` | 论述更丰满 |
| **句式升级** | `enables … to …` → `empowers … to …` / `equips … with …`；`facilitates` → `promotes` / `drives` | 词汇多样性 |
| **对比论证** | 在 P3 末尾增加 `In contrast, those who neglect … may face …` | 正反对比增强说服力 |
| **结论段深化** | `develop relevant awareness` → `cultivate a habit of …` / `engage in … on a daily basis` | 从泛泛而谈到具体行动 |
| **展望句变体** | `Were we to …` → `Only by … can we …`（倒装）/ `It is high time that …` | 丰富句型储备 |

### 3.5 完整改编示范（主题：环保 Green Lifestyle）

**P1 改编**：
> In the era of climate crisis, it is vitally important to adopt a green lifestyle. Personally, I fully agree with this view.

**P2 改编**：
> Undoubtedly, green lifestyle serves as a powerful weapon in environmental protection. For example, plenty of young people keep reducing plastic use and saving energy after work to reduce carbon footprint. Therefore, green lifestyle can help them protect the ecosystem amid worsening climate change.

**P3 改编**：
> The significance of green lifestyle manifests in two key dimensions. Firstly, regarding individual health, it enables residents to improve physical well-being, and reduce exposure to pollutants. Secondly, in the context of social sustainability, it facilitates conserving natural resources, and building an eco-friendly community, thereby demonstrating its multifaceted value.

**P4 改编**：
> In conclusion, we must realize the significance of green lifestyle. Therefore, citizens should reduce waste and recycle daily; besides, enterprises are advised to develop green technologies. Were we to make concerted efforts, the entire society could achieve further progress.

### 3.6 完整改编示范（主题：数字素养 Digital Literacy）

**P3 改编**：
> The significance of digital literacy manifests in two key dimensions. Firstly, regarding personal life, it enables ordinary citizens to access information efficiently, and avoid online fraud. Secondly, in the context of career development, it facilitates adapting to workplace digitalization, and seizing emerging opportunities, thereby demonstrating its multifaceted value.

**P4 改编**：
> In conclusion, we must realize the significance of digital literacy. Therefore, individuals should cultivate a habit of continuous learning and stay updated on new technologies; besides, educational institutions are advised to integrate digital skills training into curricula to bridge the digital divide. Were we to make concerted efforts, everyone could achieve further progress in the digital age.

# 第二编：问题解决型模板（以"抵制不良事物"为范文）

## 一、模板主题

**Resist and keep away from [不良主题]** — 抵制和远离不良事物（如成瘾、网络欺凌、不良信息等）

## 二、模板框架：双主体建议结构

该模板从 **家庭-学校** 和 **社会-政府** 两个维度，提出系统性应对不良影响的策略。

### 模板骨架

```
Resist and keep away from [不良主题]

层面一：[家长 / 老师 / 学校]

1. 尊重孩子的 [隐私 / 选择 / 决定 / 意见]
   - 尊重孩子的自主权，倾听他们的想法。

2. 给予更多的 [支持] 和 [监督]
   - 在关怀中提供引导，而非单纯控制。

3. 建议引入更多的 [课程] 和 [活动] 来帮助孩子们 [做某事]
   - 如培养健康习惯、建立自信等，通过教育和活动正面引导。

层面二：[社会 / 网络 / 媒体 / 政府]

- 要建立完善的 [监督]、[反馈]、[运行] 机制
  - 构建闭环管理体系，强调"反馈"环节。
```

### 模板英文化版本（可替换槽位）

> **On the family-school level:**
> 1. Respect children's **[privacy / choices / decisions / opinions]** .
> 2. Offer more **[support]** and **[supervision]** .
> 3. Introduce more **[courses]** and **[activities]** to help children **[do sth.]** .
>
> **On the society-government level:**
> - Establish a sound system of **[supervision]** , **[feedback]** , and **[operation]** .

## 三、问题解决型模板改编方法

### 3.1 改编步骤

| 步骤 | 操作 |
|------|------|
| 1 | 确定**不良主题**（如 Internet addiction / cyberbullying / misinformation / unhealthy lifestyle） |
| 2 | 替换**层面一槽位**：选择恰当的尊重对象（隐私/选择/意见）、支持类型、课程活动内容 |
| 3 | 替换**层面二槽位**：针对不良主题设计对应的监督机制、反馈渠道、运行体系 |
| 4 | 可扩展**层面三**（如法律/技术层面）使论述更丰满 |

### 3.2 改编示范（主题：网络成瘾 Internet Addiction）

> **On the family-school level:**
> 1. Respect children's **privacy and choices** while guiding their online behavior.
> 2. Offer more **emotional support** and **parental supervision**.
> 3. Introduce more **offline activities and sports programs** to help children **develop real-world social skills**.
>
> **On the society-government level:**
> - Establish a sound system of **content supervision, user feedback, and regulatory operation** to create a healthier online environment.

### 3.3 问题解决型 vs 观点论证型 对比

| 维度 | 观点论证型（类型一） | 问题解决型（类型二） |
|------|---------------------|---------------------|
| 核心逻辑 | 论证某事物的**重要性/意义** | 提出应对某问题的**措施/方案** |
| 结构 | 四段式：引言→举例论证→双维度分析→结论建议 | 双层面建议：家庭学校层面 + 社会政府层面 |
| 论证方式 | 举例 + 因果推导 + 双维度价值分析 | 分层建议 + 机制建设 |
| 开头句式 | In the [时代], it is vitally important to … | (需自行设计引言段) |
| 结尾句式 | Were we to make concerted efforts, … could … | 强调机制建设与闭环管理 |
