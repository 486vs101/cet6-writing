# 六级作文新类型：材料评论型（Material Comment Essay）

---

## 一、写作模板框架

### 1.1 题型特征

题目给出一段 **2~3 句的英文陈述**（描述一个社会现象或问题），要求考生对此发表评论。与传统的"名言警句型"不同，材料评论型给的是**一段完整的事实性/观点性描述**而非格言。

**真题示例材料：**
> Nowadays, many young people lack the ability to distinguish fake news, and false information misleads people and causes social problems.

### 1.2 模板框架（4 段式）

| 段落 | 功能 | 核心任务 | 建议句数 |
|------|------|----------|----------|
| **第一段** | 引入话题 + 概括材料 + 表态 | 用自己的话改写材料内容，引出话题，表明立场 | 3~4 句 |
| **第二段** | 论证观点一 | 深入分析现象的成因或危害，可"仿写"材料内容作为例子展开 | 4~5 句 |
| **第三段** | 论证观点二 / 解决建议 | 从另一角度论证，或提出应对措施 | 4~5 句 |
| **第四段** | 总结升华 | 重申观点，展望未来 | 2~3 句 |

### 1.3 模板范文（基于本次真题）

**Paragraph 1 — 引入 + 概括 + 表态**

> Currently, the topic of media literacy has triggered widespread concern and heated discussions among the public. As is stated in the given material, a growing number of young people find it hard to tell fake news from real information, and misleading content has already caused serious social problems. Personally, I fully agree with this view.

**Paragraph 2 — 分析成因 / 危害**

> To begin with, the root cause behind this phenomenon lies in the lack of systematic media education. Most young people grow up immersed in social media, where information flows in at an overwhelming speed, yet few of them have received formal training in fact-checking or source evaluation. As a result, when confronted with fabricated headlines or manipulated statistics — just as the material points out — they tend to accept them without questioning, which further amplifies the spread of misinformation.

**Paragraph 3 — 另一角度 / 解决建议**

> Moreover, the consequences of this problem extend far beyond individual judgment. False information, once widely circulated, can incite public panic, damage reputations, and even undermine social trust. To address this issue, concerted efforts are needed: schools should incorporate media literacy into the curriculum, social media platforms must strengthen content moderation, and young people themselves should cultivate a habit of cross-checking information from multiple sources.

**Paragraph 4 — 总结**

> In conclusion, the inability to distinguish fake news among the younger generation is a pressing issue that deserves immediate attention. Only through joint efforts of education, regulation, and self-discipline can we build a healthier information environment.

## 二、改编套用方法

### 2.1 模板通用句式（固定部分，直接背诵）

| 位置 | 功能 | 通用句式 |
|------|------|----------|
| 第一段引入 | 引出话题 | Currently, the topic of ______ has triggered widespread concern and heated discussions among the public. |
| 第一段概括 | 改写材料 | As is stated / revealed / indicated in the given material, ______. |
| 第一段表态 | 表明立场 | Personally, I fully agree with this view. / From my perspective, this statement captures an undeniable reality. / I partly agree, but there is more to consider. |
| 第二段开头 | 论点一 | To begin with, the root cause behind this phenomenon lies in ______. / First and foremost, this problem can be attributed to ______. |
| 第二段引用材料 | 仿写当例子 | Just as the material points out / describes / suggests, ______. |
| 第三段开头 | 论点二 | Moreover, the consequences extend far beyond ______. / Furthermore, another contributing factor is ______. |
| 第三段措施 | 解决方案 | To address this issue, concerted efforts are needed: ______ should ______; ______ must ______. |
| 第四段总结 | 重申升华 | In conclusion, ______ is a pressing issue that deserves immediate attention. Only through ______ can we ______. |

### 2.2 改编步骤（逐步替换法）

**Step 1：识别材料关键词**
阅读题干给出的英文陈述，提取 2~3 个核心关键词。

> 示例：材料提到 "young people + fake news + social problems"
> → 核心话题可抽象为：**media literacy（媒介素养）**、**information discernment（信息辨别力）**、**digital citizenship（数字公民素养）**

**Step 2：改写材料为第一段**
不要照抄原文！用以下方式改写：
- **同义词替换**：distinguish → tell apart / differentiate / discern；fake news → false information / misleading content / fabricated news
- **句式变换**：主动 → 被动；简单句 → 复合句
- **抽象概括**：把具体描述提炼为一个社会议题

**Step 3：将材料关键词融入第二段**
在第二段中引用材料作为"现实案例"，用 "Just as the material points out..." 自然过渡，使论证有据可依。

**Step 4：套用通用句式完成第三、四段**
根据材料涉及的社会问题类型，选择对应角度：
- 如果是**危害类问题** → 第三段分析后果 + 措施
- 如果是**成因类问题** → 第三段补充另一成因
- 如果是**两方观点型** → 第三段讨论反方观点

## 四、类型特点总结

### 4.1 与既有类型的区别

| 对比维度 | 名言警句型 | 图表描述型 | **材料评论型（新）** |
|----------|-----------|-----------|---------------------|
| 题干形式 | 一句格言/谚语 | 图表 + 数据 | 一段 2~3 句英文陈述 |
| 第一段任务 | 解释名言含义 | 描述图表趋势 | **概括改写材料 + 表态** |
| 核心能力 | 抽象思维、举例论证 | 数据描述、趋势分析 | **信息提炼、评述能力** |
| 难点 | 对名言理解偏差 | 数据表达不准确 | **照抄材料扣分，改写不到位也扣分** |

### 4.2 关键避坑指南

1. **禁止整句照抄材料**：第一段必须用自己的话改写，同义词替换 + 句式变换是必须的
2. **材料至少被引用一次**：在第二段中用 "Just as the material points out..." 巧妙地"仿写当例子"
3. **表态不能含糊**：agree / disagree / partly agree 三选一，不要写 "it depends"
4. **话题要适当抽象**：材料讲 fake news → 你的主题词要用 media literacy / information discernment 等上位概念，体现学术词汇量
